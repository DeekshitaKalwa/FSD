Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are included in the plans?

Each plan offers access to a specific set of courses. The basic plan includes introductory courses, while the standard and premium plans offer access to more advanced content, projects, and additional learning resources.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time by contacting our support team or through your user dashboard.

Do I get a certificate after completing the course?

Yes, students who complete the Premium plan get a certificate of completion that can be shared with potential employers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers.
